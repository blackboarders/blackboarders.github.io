Adjectives for Physical Descriptions

Exercise:
Look at the pictures and choose the correct adjective to describe each one.



Answers

1. My father is tall
2. My grandmother is old
3. My sister is young

Using "To Be" with Adjectives

Exercise:
Complete the sentences with the correct form of 'to be':

1. She ___ beautiful.
2. They ___ happy.
3. I ___ tired.

Answers

1. is
2. are
3. am

Practice with "To Be" and Adjectives

Exercise:
Look at the pictures and write sentences using 'to be' and the correct adjective:



(Picture of a big cat)
(Picture of a happy child)
(Picture of a short man)

Answers

1. The cat is big.
2. The child is happy.
3. The man is short.

Demonstrative Pronouns: This, That, These, Those

Exercise:
Choose the correct demonstrative pronoun for each sentence:

1. ___ is my pen. (near)
2. ___ is her house. (far)
3. ___ are your books. (near)
4. ___ are their bags. (far)

Answers

1. This
2. That
3. These
4. Those

Using Demonstrative Pronouns in Sentences

Exercise:
Write sentences using the correct demonstrative pronoun and noun:

1. (Close) ___ is a book.
2. (Distant) ___ are mountains.
3. (Close) ___ are children.
4. (Distant) ___ is a cat.

Answers

1. This
2. Those
3. These
4. That

Listening Practice

Let’s listen to someone describing people and things.
Pay attention to how they use adjectives, the verb 'to be,' and demonstrative pronouns.



Exercise:
After listening, answer these questions:

1. What color is the dog?
2. What size is the house?
3. What are the books like?
4. How are the neighbors described?

Answers

1. Brown
2. Big
3. Old and interesting
4. Friendly
